When to use

The hero baner should typically only be used on the home page of your product/service. It's designed to contain key content describing what the product offers along with the key interaction you wish users to have with your product.

The markup provided includes the search feature based on the gov.uk homepage search. If you do not require this simply remove the form section and the corresponding SCSS from the snippet

Try to avoid placing too much content within the banner itself. Users typicaly expect more content on a page to sit below it. Having some of that visible on mobile and desktop devices is advisable.
