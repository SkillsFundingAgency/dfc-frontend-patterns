When to use

The 3 colum content grid should be used for simplistic content. Anything within the grid will be split evenly between all columns, this therefore lends itself well to lists or text based content.

The class can be applied to a list or wrapping div. With a list being used in the live example.

Do not use this snippet for overall page layouts, it will not allow you to control what appears in which column.

Research

Research findings

This section was designed with the need in mind that users only have a rough idea of what they would like to do and after exploring their mental models when they are in those situations. We conducted research to ensure that we explore how users expect to find relevant careers information and their mental models and explored the idea of categories.

Research methods

After finding out more about how they associate different careers in sectors, we did an open card sort to understand how people group them together and what category names we come up with. At a next stage, after collaborating with the team we agreed on a number of category names and did a closed card sort and a tree test to validate our options. We reached a stage where we are satisfied in terms of navigation and how people explore job categories as well as they position in relation to search. We picked the 3 columns as the users were more comfortable browsing through and find what they were interested.
