When to use

The feedback survey banner should be used as a way to direct users to a feedback form or page. It has been designed to appear until dismissed and so can therefore be used across all pages on your service if required.

Dependencies

The survey banner relies on the GOVUK cookie javascript. This should be packaged as part of the GOV.UK Template, without this the banner will be permenantly hidden. You can remove the display: none property from its container if you wish to display it without JS.

Research

Background

The feedback survey banner was designed to ensure it's prompt. easy to take action and close it. The pattern has different colour which matches with our user's preferences in relation to careers advice and getting away from the GDS pattern of white background. In addition the banner has a darker hue to increase prominence in relation to the colour of the 1st section.

Research findings

The feedback survey has been tested in lab 1-1 sessions over it's layout, content, information architecture, interactivity and discoverability. In addition we are aware that about 1% of users in public beta have clicked on it and went through the survey.
