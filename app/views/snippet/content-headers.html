When to use

This snippet has been designed as an alternative to the default GDS section headers. It provides better clarity to subsections of content within a key area, allowing for large ammounts of content to be scanned and consumed more quickly by the user.

It should only be used when the default GDS headings have been tested and proven to make content overwhelming or difficult to understand.
