When to use

The redesigned GDS jumplinks should only typically be used in places where that standard GDS link list design doesn't envoke enough user engagement. This updated design should always be tested against the original to provide proof that the updated version is beneficial when your product is assessed.

Research

In order to get to a conclusive state that this design provided the best engagement for users, without distracting from the content itself, it went through AB testing on the BAU service. The details and results of those can be found on confluence: https://skillsfundingagency.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/DFC/pages/324698399/A+B+Testing+Plan+Sprint+26+27
