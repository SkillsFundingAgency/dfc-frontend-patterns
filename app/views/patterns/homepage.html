When to use

We've used this pattern as our homepage, to allow users easy navigation through the service. This is helpful for our user group which come to the National Careers Service requiring assistance, but are not sure exactly what they need from us, and what tools we offer.

Research

Background

We often see users visit our site because they are told of the National Careers Service as a service which can help them move forward in their career path. We've seen typically with users who had visited our previous homepage, which had a static list of links to our different products, that they found the homepage disengaging and didn't serve their needs.

Research findings

We tested different variants of patterns of navigation and research pointed to the rings functioning the best and being most engaging for users. We learnt it was found as 'easy to navigate' by users, and the word 'easy' was repeated in several research sessions we held. Further information is available on Confluence within the findings for sprint 21 and sprint 23.
